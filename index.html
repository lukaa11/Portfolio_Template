I'am Łukasz

My name is Łukasz Kuraś. I was born on May 18, 1994 in Zawiercie, I am 26 years old and currently live in Siewierz.


In 2002 I started studying at the Primary School No. Duchy of Siewierz in Siewierz. I continued my education at the gymnasium in the same school. In 2011, after passing the junior high school exam, I entered the secondary school im. John Paul II in Siewierz. In 20013, I passed my secondary school-leaving examination in subjects such as basic mathematics and enlargement, Polish and English. In October of the same year I started studying engineering in Gliwice at the Silesian University of Technology in the field of Power Engineering. In 2017, I defended my engineering thesis and started my master's degree in Electrical Engineering at the same university. In September 2019, I defended my master's thesis and graduated from the Silesian University of Technology in Gliwice at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering.


6 months ago, I started learning frontend web development. I developed my skills on the basis of courses on the YouTube platform, Passion of IT and Coders Lab. I also gained knowledge in the field of Backend. I am currently implementing the Frontend developer course as part of the "My zone of influence 2.0" project.


In my free time I do jogging, which has taught me to be systematic, patient and act according to the plan. Running allows me to find a space to consider my professional challenges and make the right business decisions.
